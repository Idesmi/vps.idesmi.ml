What's a computer Waifu?

According to the Urban Dictionary,

the term "waifu" is used to refer to a fictional girl or woman (usually in Anime, Manga, or video-games) that you have sexual attraction to, and you would even marry.

This is obviously the first entry, as UB is not shy in providing much more meaningful insights. Ol' good internet vocabulary.

The second source of our renewed knowledge would be Know Your Meme, which reads, in a behaved tone:

[...] the term has been [] adopted by Otaku circles and anime fans to refer to one's favorite female manga or anime character.

If you're embarassed at the idea of linking or even falling in love with a fictional character, then this second definition definitely is the only one you should read.
